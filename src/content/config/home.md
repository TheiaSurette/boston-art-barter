# About
**Do you make art? Do you want to meet other people who make art? Would you even maybe want to trade your art for theirs? If so meet us on Sunday, April 19th by Brewer fountain in the Boston Common from 2-6pm!**

# F.A.Q.
**do you have to be an artist?**
no! anyone is welcome! you can trade services and skills or just come to hang out!

**is the form required?**
no! you can just show up! but if you fill out the form you can join the instagram gc to talk about the event, and it also has an option to fill out info abt a specific artwork you want to trade and i’ll print out a ticket with the info and image you input! i’ll also have blank forms at the event as well if you want to fill it out there

**will this event happen again?** 
maybe! that depends on how it goes :P but the gc and website will stay live after the event is over

**what is the form for?**
a bunch of things! so i can get an idea of attendance numbers, to join the instagram gc, to submit info so i can print artwork ticket to give to you at the event, to add info about you to be added to a directory of the artists that participated that will be on the website after the event

**do i need to be there for the start?**
no, you can show up whenever!

**will there be food?**
maybe! bring a few bucks for pizza and if enough ppl do we can order some :D

**what if i don’t have something to trade?**
please come anyways and make some new friends! maybe you’ll get an idea or maybe someone will want to trade for something you didn’t even think of

**what can be traded?**
anything other than money! art pieces, commissions, services (tattoos, welding, haircuts, dog walking, etc) and like literally anything else you can think of

**what do i need to bring?**
just yourself! you can bring art to trade if you want to and its portable but it’s not necessary! if you fill out the form i’ll print out a ticket with the info and image you input but if you don’t want to do that you can just show ppl pictures on your phone or just talk to people

**what if i have nothing to trade?**
come anyways! even if you don’t have anythin**g you want to trade you can come meet other artists, build community, and make friends!
